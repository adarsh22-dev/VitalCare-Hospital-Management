VitalCare AI - Hospital Management System (AI-HMS)

VitalCare is a comprehensive, AI-powered Hospital Management System designed to streamline medical operations, enhance patient care, and provide intelligent insights for administrators.

Core Features

Patient Management
Digital Patient Profiles: Complete medical history, allergy tracking, and chronic disease tagging.
Smart Search: Filter patients by condition, status, or ID.
Registration: Streamlined OPD/IPD and Emergency registration flows.

Appointment & Scheduling
Conflict Detection: Real-time AI monitoring of scheduling overlaps for doctors and patients.
Smart Slot Management: Optimized booking based on doctor availability and specialty.
Reminders: Automated SMS/Email reminders for upcoming appointments.

Bed & Ward Management
Real-time Availability: Track ICU, General, and Private bed status.
Auto-Assignment: AI-suggested bed allocation based on patient condition.
Cleaning Status: Monitor room readiness for new admissions.

Pharmacy Management
Inventory Tracking: Real-time stock monitoring with expiry alerts.
Prescription Billing: Direct integration with patient prescriptions for accurate billing.
Auto-Reorder: Intelligent alerts when stock levels are low.

Laboratory Management
Test Tracking: Monitor lab tests from sample collection to result upload.
AI Analysis: Highlighting of abnormal results for immediate doctor attention.
PDF Reports: Automated generation of professional lab reports.

Billing & Finance
Integrated Billing: Combined billing for OPD, IPD, Pharmacy, and Lab services.
Insurance Integration: Tracking of claims and insurance approvals.
Financial Analytics: Revenue dashboards with daily and monthly growth trends.

AI-Powered Modules

VitalAssist AI: A professional medical assistant for administrative tasks and symptom analysis.
AI Diagnosis Assistant: Predictive modeling for disease probability based on symptoms.
Conflict Auto-Detection: Real-time monitoring of staff workload and scheduling overlaps.
Smart Doctor Matching: AI-driven staff assignment based on specialty and patient needs.

Tech Stack

Frontend: React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, Recharts.
Backend: Node.js, Express, Better-SQLite3.
API: Google Gemini API (@google/genai).
Icons: Lucide React.

Security & Compliance

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Secure access for doctors, admins, and staff.
Data Encryption: Patient records are stored securely with audit logs.
HIPAA Ready: Designed with global healthcare standards in mind.
